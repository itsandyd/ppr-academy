## üé¨ Production Tips

### For All Content:
1. **Use real screenshots** from the dashboard (blur sensitive data)
2. **Add motion graphics** for key metrics/numbers
3. **Include captions** (80% watch without sound)
4. **Brand consistently** with PPR Academy colors/logos
5. **Clear CTA** in every piece (link in bio, sign up, learn more)

### Content Calendar Suggestion:
- **Monday:** Motivational/Inspirational (Features 3, 4, 9)
- **Wednesday:** Educational (Features 2, 6, 8, 12)
- **Friday:** Feature Showcase (Features 1, 5, 7, 10)
- **Weekend:** Behind-the-scenes (Features 13, 14)

## üìä Success Metrics to Track

For each piece of content, monitor:
- **Reach:** Views, impressions
- **Engagement:** Likes, comments, shares, saves
- **Click-through:** Link clicks, profile visits
- **Conversion:** Sign-ups attributed to content
- **Retention:** Average watch time (video content)

### Benchmarks (adjust based on your audience size):
- **Good:** 3-5% engagement rate
- **Great:** 5-10% engagement rate
- **Viral:** 10%+ engagement rate
