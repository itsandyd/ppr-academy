## ðŸ’¡ PRO TIPS

### **Editing Tips:**
1. **Quick Cuts:** Keep shots to 2-3 seconds max
2. **Beat Sync:** Time cuts to music beat
3. **Text Animation:** Fade in, not slide (cleaner)
4. **Transitions:** Simple cuts > fancy transitions
5. **B-Roll:** Always have backup footage

### **Voiceover Tips:**
1. **Energy:** Speak with enthusiasm (you're excited!)
2. **Pace:** Slightly faster than normal speech
3. **Clarity:** Enunciate clearly
4. **Pauses:** Strategic pauses for emphasis
5. **Recording:** Use good mic (even phone headphones work)

### **Engagement Hacks:**
1. **Pin Top Comment:** Pin a comment asking a question
2. **Reply Fast:** First hour engagement is critical
3. **Ask Questions:** In caption and on-screen
4. **Controversy:** "Is [competitor] actually worth it?" (carefully)
5. **Tag Accounts:** Tag relevant accounts (tools you mention)
