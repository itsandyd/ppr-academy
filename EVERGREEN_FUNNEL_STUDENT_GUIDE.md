## ğŸ¯ Weekly Challenges

As you progress through the course, complete these hands-on challenges:

### Week 1: Foundation Setup (After Module 1)

**Challenge 1: Create Your Lead Magnet**
1. Go to `/store/[storeId]/lead-magnets`
2. Click "Create Lead Magnet"
3. Options:
   - Free sample pack
   - First module of your course
   - Mixing cheatsheet PDF
   - Beat-making guide
4. Set up auto-send confirmation email
5. Get your lead magnet URL

**Challenge 2: Build Welcome Sequence**
1. Go to `/store/[storeId]/email`
2. Click "New Campaign"
3. Select "Welcome" template
4. Customize with your voice
5. Schedule 3 emails:
   - Day 1: Welcome + deliver lead magnet
   - Day 3: Introduce yourself + share value
   - Day 7: Pitch your course/product
6. Activate automation

**Challenge 3: Connect Instagram**
1. Go to `/store/[storeId]/social`
2. Click "Connect Instagram"
3. Authorize your Business account
4. Set up first DM automation:
   - Trigger: Someone comments "FREE"
   - Action: Auto-send DM with lead magnet link
5. Test it!

**Success Metric:** 10+ new email subscribers by end of Week 1

### Week 2: Diagnose & Optimize (After Module 2)

**Challenge 4: Find Your Bottleneck**
1. Go to `/store/[storeId]/analytics`
2. Click "Drop-off Points"
3. Identify where most people leave your funnel
4. Common bottlenecks:
   - High cart abandonment?
   - Low email open rates?
   - People stop at Chapter 3 of your course?

**Challenge 5: Create Recovery Campaign**
1. Go to `/store/[storeId]/email`
2. Create "Re-engagement" campaign
3. Target people who hit your bottleneck
4. Example for abandoned cart:
   - Subject: "Still interested in [Course Name]?"
   - Body: Address common objection + testimonial + discount
5. Track if bottleneck improves

**Challenge 6: A/B Test Subject Lines**
1. In Email Campaigns, click "A/B Test"
2. Create 2 versions:
   - Subject A: "How to [Benefit]"
   - Subject B: "[Number] Ways to [Result]"
3. Send to 50% each
4. Winner auto-sends to rest of list
5. Note which performed better

**Success Metric:** 5% increase in funnel completion rate

### Week 3: Level Up Your Fans (After Module 3)

**Challenge 7: Tag Your Fans by Level**
1. Go to `/store/[storeId]/contacts`
2. Import your existing email list (if any)
3. Tag each fan by engagement level:
   - **Discovery:** New subscribers (0-7 days)
   - **Social Proof:** Clicked testimonial link
   - **Engagers:** Opened 3+ emails
   - **True Fans:** Purchased once
   - **Super Fans:** Purchased 2+ times
4. View total count per level

**Challenge 8: Create Level-Specific Campaigns**
1. Create 5 separate email campaigns:
   - **Discovery:** Welcome + lead magnet
   - **Social Proof:** Case study + testimonials
   - **Engagers:** Exclusive content + community invite
   - **True Fans:** Upsell to next product
   - **Super Fans:** VIP access + referral request
2. Send to segmented lists
3. Track which level has highest engagement

**Challenge 9: Enable Social Proof**
1. Go to course settings
2. Enable "Reviews & Ratings"
3. Send email to past students: "Can you leave a review?"
4. Display top 3 reviews on course landing page
5. Add testimonials to sales emails

**Success Metric:** Identify your top 3 Super Fans

## ğŸ“ Pro Tips for Success

### 1. Start Small, Scale Fast
Don't try to build everything at once. Complete one challenge per day. By Week 4, you'll have a complete funnel.

### 2. Real Data > Guesses
Always check your Analytics before making decisions. Let the data guide your strategy, not assumptions.

### 3. Iterate Based on Results
Your first email won't be perfect. Track open rates, click rates, and conversion rates. Improve what's underperforming.

### 4. Automate Everything You Can
The goal is a funnel that runs without you. Set up automations once, then let them generate fans on autopilot.

### 5. Test Everything
A/B test subject lines, email copy, lead magnet offers, and social posts. Small improvements compound over time.
